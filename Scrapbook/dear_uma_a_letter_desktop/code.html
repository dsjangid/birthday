A Letter

For Uma

The Fifteenth of July

Dearest Shortiee,

I've been trying to write this for so long... and honestly, nothing ever feels enough. Every time I think I've written what I feel, it still feels like there's so much left unsaid. I don't think you realise how much you've become a part of my life. Somewhere between all our conversations, random moments, stupid arguments, laughter, and everything we've been through... you became home to me. You're the person I want to tell everything to, the one I look for first, and the one who somehow makes even an ordinary day feel special just by being in it.

I remember the quiet mornings, the way the light hit the kitchen floor while you laughed at something only you could see. I remember the milestones, yes, but it is these smaller, silent threads that weave the most beautiful tapestry.

A summer Afternoon 3rd of July

One thing I'll always admire about you is your heart. The way you care, the way you love, the way you always try... even when it's hard. I just wish you could see yourself the way I do sometimes, because then maybe you'd finally understand why I adore you so much.

On your birthday, I don't just wish you happiness. I wish you peace. I hope you smile more, overthink a little less, and never forget how deeply you're loved. I hope this year gives you every little thing you've been quietly hoping for, because you deserve that and so much more.

With all my love,

Always yours.. Wormiee✨
